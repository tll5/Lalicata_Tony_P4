Top Travel Wonders Far from Home!



The Fjords of Norway

Norway is home to over 1000 unique fjords that are practically untouched by civilization. Being that Norway is such a large country in size compared to its human population, and that most of its population lives in or near Oslo to the south, most of the northern regions are largely uninhabited. For all those brave, ambitious hikers out there, visiting the northern reaches of Norway and its steep fjords is just the right adventure for you.
