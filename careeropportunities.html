University of Oxford

Often ranked amongst the highest universities across the globe, University of Oxford in the United Kingdom takes in only 10% of student applicants each admission cycle, and almost half of their entire student body consists of international students! Whether you are a young adult looking to do top-level research at the University, a student from another part of the world looking for the best education, or both along with a desire to travel and live in the UK, studying at University of Oxford is an opportunity like no other.
